About Me

hey there, i'm kushagra. i'm a linux and free software enthusiast currently cruising on arch linux. i discovered linux at a young age, and it's been a genuine journey of self-discovery and advocacy ever since.

i have a passion for breaking the norms while staying true to what matters. i enjoy self-hosting services and take pride in contributing to communities like openstreetmaps. i also support projects such as signal and tor because i believe in the power of privacy and digital freedom for everyone.

when i'm not immersed in the world of code, i love diving into literature, psychology, and philosophy. these interests inspire me to see beyond the obvious and appreciate life in all its complexity.

welcome to my little corner of the web—a space where curiosity is nurtured. thanks for stopping by, and i hope you leave here inspired to challenge the norms in your own way. have a good day <3
